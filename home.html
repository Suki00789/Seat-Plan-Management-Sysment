About Seat Plan Management System


Seat Plan Management System is a system for teachers. Through this, they can planned seat automatically. After completing the seat plan, they will be able to convert it to excel and be able to edit it.

After editing, they will be able to upload it to students. So that, students can download and see the seat plan before the examination.
